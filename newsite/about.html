Most “web designers” hand you a template and wave goodbye. I build the thing that actually runs your business.

Hi, I'm Maggie. I work with small businesses of all kinds — contractors, home services, studios, shops, and growing brands — and I kept seeing the same thing: a good-looking site that doesn't actually do anything. No real booking, no way to manage clients, nothing the owner can run day to day.

So I build the other kind. Polished, professional sites and web apps backed by real, custom code — with online booking that sends each client to the right place, and private dashboards to manage appointments, data, and the day-to-day in one spot.

I handle everything from the look and feel to the build and launch, and I work with you, start to finish — so what you get is polished, genuinely useful, and completely yours. No page builders. No locked-in platforms. Just real code you own.
